2026 Convention Access Scholarship

101st NBA Annual Convention · Nashville, Tennessee

Join hundreds of legal professionals from across the country for a week of networking, mentorship, professional development, and unforgettable experiences at the 101st National Bar Association Annual Convention. In this inaugural year of the scholarship, several recipients will be selected — each receiving sponsored registration, five nights of hotel accommodations, and up to $500 in flight reimbursement.

5 nights hotel (Sat–Thu)
