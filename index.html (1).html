Why attend

Why this matters

One week that can change your trajectory

The NBA Annual Convention is where the nation's Black legal community gathers — judges, partners, general counsel, and the students who will join their ranks. This scholarship puts you in the room.

Build a career-long network

Meet practicing attorneys, recruiters, and judges face to face. The connections you make here become mentors, references, and the people who think of you when a position opens.

Earn CLE and sharpen your craft

Sit in on substantive programming across practice areas — from trial advocacy to emerging law — and graduate convention week knowing more than you did walking in.

Opportunities like this don't come around often. Show up, contribute, and let the profession get to know you. Sponsored · Nashville · July 25–31, 2026
