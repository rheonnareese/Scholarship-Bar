About this scholarship

About this scholarship

Support when it matters most

Recipients are selected based on demonstrated financial need and commitment to advancing equity in the legal profession. Here's what to know before you apply.

What the funds cover

Bar preparation course fees, examination registration, study materials, and other costs that support your bar prep.

Who's eligible

Current NBA Law Student Division members in good standing, enrolled in or graduating from law school, and registered to sit for the July 2026 bar in any U.S. jurisdiction.
